Banana Bread

God's gift in the form of bread.

If you haven't had (good) banana bread then it's most certainly a must eat recipe. Banana bread is not only amazing, but it will make your spunk taste good too!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. prepeat oven to 175C. Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. In a large bowl cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir Banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 60~65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
